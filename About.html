Autobiography

Matthew Du is an Industrial Engineering student currently studying at the University of Toronto, with a self-initiated minor in Computer Science (the one offered by the Faculty of Arts and Science). Matthew is interested in a wide various different topics and disciplines within Industrial Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Computer Science (although he cannot claim to be an expert in any of these), such as,

  • Computational Complexity and Algorithmic Graph Theory
  • Fluid Dynamics
  • Mechatronics and Robotics
  • Systems and Software Development

Outside of his curricular activities, Matthew is an avid musical performer and arranger/composer with various musical groups in the GTA. His primary instrument is Violin, but he is also proficient in various other instruments, such as Viola, Clarinet, Bass Clarinet, Saxophone and many more. He currently preforms with the U of T Engineering Skule Orchestra as a second violin player.

Aside from music, Matthew is also enjoys watching and running in Track and Field (although less and less over the years), solving Rubiks Cubes, and watching food videos on YouTube and trying new recipes in his Kitchen.

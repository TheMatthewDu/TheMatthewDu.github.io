Co-President External and Co-Founder

I am the co-founder and current co-president of external affairs of the Canadian Society of Mechanical Engineers (CSME) Student Chapter at the University of Toronto. I conceived of the idea of forming this organization as I noticed that there was a professional development student organization for all other engineering disciplines at the University of Toronto, but not for mechanical engineering students. This organization strives to help mechanical engineering students develop their technical and networking skills through alumni engagements, industry events, and technical skills seminars and competitions. Within my role as President of External affairs, I am responsible for finding and connecting with external organizations and alumni to host events with them throughout the year.

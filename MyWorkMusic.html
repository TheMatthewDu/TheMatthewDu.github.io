Performances

The following are recordings of various performances of mine with chamber music groups at A.Y. Jackson Secondary School in Toronto, Canada

Violin

This is a recording of the forth movement of Dvořák's String Quartet #12, Op. 96 (American), in which I played first violin for. The recording was taken in the December 2019 at the Winter Music Night at A.Y. Jackson Secondary School in Toronto.

Viola

This is a recording of Joe Hisashi's Merry Go Around of Life from the anime Howl's Moving Castle, in which I played viola for. The recording was taken in February 2020 at the Chamber Night at A.Y. Jackson Secondary School in Toronto.

Clarinet/Bass Clarinet

This is a recording of an arrangement of Franz Schubert's Erlkönig by Ioan Dobrinescu, in which I played Bass Clarinet for. The recording was taken in February 2020 at the Chamber Night at A.Y. Jackson Secondary School in Toronto.
